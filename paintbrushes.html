Paintbrushes


How to use

  • Dip in paint
  • Hold like a pencil
  • Paint your object with small strokes
  • When done or changing colors put dirty brush in clean cup of water

How to clean

  • Grab dirty brushes and go to a sink
  • Put small dab of soap in palm of your hand and turn on the water
  • gently rub brushes in your palm with the soap in a circular motion
  • while rubbing in circular motion run under water
  • when there is no more soap left put them aside to air dry
